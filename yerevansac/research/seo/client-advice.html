Tips for Taking Great Work Photos

  • Capture the 'Steam and Sizzle': Take close-up photos of kebabs and grilled meats while they are still steaming to convey freshness.
  • The 'Cheese Pull' Shot: For Adjaruli Khachapuri, take a short video or photo of the cheese and egg being mixed to drive high engagement.
  • Show the Craft: Photograph the 'work in progress' of chefs hand-folding Khinkali dumplings to emphasize the handmade nature of the food.
  • Atmosphere and Lighting: Use natural daylight for food shots near windows and warm, dim lighting for evening cocktail and group dining shots.
  • Team in Action: Take photos of your staff interacting warmly with guests or preparing cocktails to build trust with the Carmichael community.
  • Plating Perfection: Ensure the background is clean and use vibrant garnishes to make the Mediterranean colors pop against the plate.

What Makes a Great Review

Feature reviews on your website that include:

  • Mentions specific menu items like 'Lula Kebab', 'Khinkali', or 'Adjaruli Khachapuri'.
  • Includes the location 'Carmichael' or 'Sacramento area' within the text.
  • Contains high-quality photos of the food or the restaurant interior.
  • Describes the service quality and the 'authentic' atmosphere of the Armenian-Georgian kitchen.
  • Highlights specific use cases like 'group dining', 'date night', or 'catering'.
  • Uses keywords related to the cuisine type such as 'Mediterranean' or 'Middle Eastern grill'.
  • The reviewer has a 'Local Guide' badge or a history of credible reviews.
